information about Harley-Davidson

Harley-Davidson is an iconic American motorcycle manufacturer and lifestyle brand headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Founded in 1903, the company is globally recognized for its heavyweight cruiser and touring motorcycles, as well as its loyal community and distinctive “potato” engine sound. There are lots of different models surrounding this manufacturer and they all come in different styles, colors and all the added bells and whistles. They have their own line of apparel and leathers and accessories for not only you but your bike too.. .

Events with community

Events that surround this community and are done with thousands of bikes each year, the main one would be The Trail of Tears Commemorative Motorcycle Ride. This is an annual event that honors the memory and legacy of the thousands of Native Americans forcibly removed from their ancestral homelands in the 1830s. It is one of the largest organized motorcycle rides in the world, often drawing over 100,000 participants. There are also lots of bike fests throughout the year in different states, but the biggest one would have to be Bike Fest down in FL each year. There you can see so many different unique bikes and some have been built from the ground up by some very talented people..
